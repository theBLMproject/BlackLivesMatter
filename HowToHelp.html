Vote

Your voice matters. You can make a difference in the world. The causes and issues you care about will not change unless you vote.

Register to vote at: www.usa.gov/register-to-vote

Write to your Government Officials

Writing to your government officials is an effective method of advocating for a cause and can even persuade your representative to vote in your favor. Here are some tips from the ACLU on writing to your representatives:
- Write a letter, letters are more personal than emails and therefore more effective
- Keep it brief, don't write letters more than one page long and focus on just one issue
- Be straight forward, say who you are and what you're writing about in th first paragraph
- Choose the 3 strongest points that will be most effective in persuading legislators to support your position
- Make it personal, write about how it will affect your community
- Make it even more personal, if you voted, supported, or donated to your representative, bring that up in your letter, the closer your legestlator feels to you, the more effective your argument will be

Find your government officials at: https://www.usa.gov/elected-officials
